Food reservation
  1. OpenTable: A widely used platform where you can easily search for restaurants by cuisine, location, price range, and availability. Many top NYC restaurants use OpenTable for reservations.
  2. Resy: Another popular reservation service for trendy and high-demand restaurants. Many of New York’s top dining spots, especially newer ones, use Resy.
  3. Yelp Reservations: Some restaurants use Yelp’s reservation feature, which allows you to book directly from their Yelp page.
  4. Bookatable: A great platform for finding restaurants that accept reservations in advance.
  5. Contact the Restaurant Directly If you know the specific restaurant you'd like to visit, you can always call them directly. Many restaurants in New York City are busy, so it’s a good idea to reserve early, especially for popular spots.
